Where it all began...

born in the wonderful country Germany,

My first ever success as an Engineer was when I build a bow with the help of my father. After I felt the pleasure of completing successfully my first project, I continued working on bigger and more time-consuming projects. The era of Mustafa's gaming world

Yes, that's right, my next step towards a better engineer was gaming. My cousin introduced me to several shooter and role play games and I was fascinated. Travel to the UK

A long time has passed, and I am now standing in front of my teachers ready to ask them for advice. Advice for what? I want to travel to the UK and improve my English skills, but I don't know where to start. I did all the preparation needed to travel to the UK. I decided to work as an Au-pair whilst visiting a school to improve my language skills.

I landed, was introduced to the city, the family, the culture, and my new environment. After approximately three months of working with the family, we agreed that the family does not necessarily need an Au-Pair, so I moved on. As I still wanted to achieve my goal and improve my language skills, I wanted to continue visiting the school I was going to. I needed a new place to stay and a new place to work. A friend of mine introduced me to someone who owns a kebab shop, I talked to him about my goals and my current situation and he did not hesitate long. I found a place for me to stay, taught me everything from scratch to work in his shop and I still visited the school.
