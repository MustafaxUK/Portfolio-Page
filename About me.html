Where it all began...

born in the wonderful country Germany,

I started my career as an Engineer from a very young age. Being curious all the time and trying to understand the world and everything in it, my spare time was always spent building things with sticks, stones and ropes. My first ever success as an Engineer was when I build a bow with the help of my father. After I felt the pleasure of completing successfully my first project, I continued working on bigger and more time-consuming projects. In a group of 5 we found a place in the woods, started to collect materials and tools together and build our own treehouse.

Where it continued...

the moment I just thought my engineering career was at it's peak, my father taught me different.

My father surpised me with a laptop, to help me study and discover the world wide web. Of course, I did not know how to use it all, so my cousin came over and taught me everything to get started. The first time checking images on Google, watching videos on Youtube and playing games in the internet, I soon started my next step to become a better engineer.
